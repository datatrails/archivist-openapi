Identity and Access Management (v1alpha1)
-----------------------------------------

Organisational access to the various resources in Jitsuin Archivist
is managed by the subjects and access_policies endpoints.

The subjects endpoint manages the granting of access to third parties.
The access_policies endpoint manages which rights have been granted.
